What is Pilates?

Pilates is a method of strength training, developed by Joseph Pilates. It focuses on core strength and movement of the spine. Core strength helps to develop stability throughout the core muscles in the body. Pilates is based on 6 core principles; Centering, Concentration, Control, Precision, Breath and Flow.

What are the benefits of practicing Pilates?

  • Improved Posture
  • Better Balance
  • More Flexibility
  • Prevent Injury
  • Body Awareness
  • Higher Energy
