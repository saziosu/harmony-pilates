Our Studio

Established in 2018, Harmony believes in making a relaxed and accessible atmosphere for everyone. We are passionate about delivering a premium experience to all clients regardless of age or experience levels. Here at Harmony we deliver a premium experience to every client that takes time out of their day to move with us. We offer both reformer and mat classes in our state-of-the-art studio, with options for custom classes to suit your needs. For more information please view our services. Our team consists of our founder, Marta, and three experienced instructors who are committed to bringing you the best pilates experience possible.
